E-Commerce Order Pipeline (Amazon-like)

When user places order:

Event:
OrderPlaced
Consumers:
Payment Service
Inventory Service
Shipping Service
Notification Service
Analytics Service

User Places Order
    ↓
Order Service publishes OrderPlaced
    ↓
Kafka Topic: order-events
    ↓
Consumers react independently



FAANG Insight:

At Amazon:

Order Service publishes OrderPlaced
Multiple downstream services consume
Services scale independently
Retry queues handle failures

This reduces latency and coupling.
